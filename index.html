Frequently Asked Questions

What is Pill Roulette?

Pill Roulette, also known as the Side Effects Game, is a horror strategy game where two players participate in a dangerous medical trial. Players take turns consuming pills with unknown effects while trying to survive and outlast their opponent.

Is Pill Roulette free to play?

Yes! Pill Roulette is completely free to play online. You can play it directly in your browser without any downloads or registration required.

How is Pill Roulette similar to Buckshot Roulette?

Both games feature high-stakes decision-making and survival elements. While Buckshot Roulette uses a shotgun, Pill Roulette uses pharmaceutical pills with various side effects, creating a unique medical trial atmosphere.

What platforms can I play Pill Roulette on?

Pill Roulette is available on HTML5 (web browsers) and Windows. The web version can be played on any device with a modern browser, including PC, Mac, and mobile devices.
